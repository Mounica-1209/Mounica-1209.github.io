About Me

Hi, I'm Mounica, originally from Hyderabad, India. My career has always been rooted in engineering, AI, and technology, but what has consistently driven me is curiosity — understanding how systems work, identifying patterns, and connecting ideas to solve meaningful problems.

I began my journey with a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Sri City, before joining SpringML where I worked on AI/ML solutions for clients including Google, Northwell Health, and U.S. state governments. At GlobalLogic, I contributed to large-scale data engineering and AI initiatives for Apple and T-Mobile.

I became increasingly interested in influencing what should be built and why — which motivated me to pursue an MBA at SMU Cox School of Business. Most recently, I worked as an AI Product & Strategy Intern at RaidoHub, contributing to predictive analytics for airport operations.
