About Me

Hi, I'm Mounica, originally from Hyderabad, India. My career has always been rooted in engineering, AI, and technology, driven by curiosity to understand how systems work and connect ideas to solve meaningful problems.

I began with a B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Sri City, then joined SpringML working on AI/ML for Google, Northwell Health, and U.S. state governments. At GlobalLogic, I led data engineering and AI for Apple and T-Mobile.

Wanting to influence what gets built and why, I pursued a STEM MBA at SMU Cox School of Business. Most recently I was an AI Product and Strategy Intern at RaidoHub, delivering predictive analytics for airport operations.
