Finance
ACCT 6201
Accounting
Covers the fundamentals of financial accounting — ledger recording, financial statement preparation, and ratio analysis. Builds the ability to read and interpret balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow reports.
Why It MattersUnderstanding accounting ensures you can evaluate performance, support budgeting decisions, and communicate effectively with finance teams at any level.
Strategy
STRA — Advanced
Advanced Strategy
Explores sophisticated competitive dynamics, corporate strategy, and long-term value creation. Includes case studies on market entry, diversification, and strategic commitment at the enterprise level.
Why It MattersEssential for high-level strategic leadership — provides the frameworks to make decisions that shape a company's competitive position over years, not quarters.
Marketing

AI Marketing
Studies the application of artificial intelligence in marketing functions including personalization, analytics, and automation. Examines how AI improves targeting, campaign optimization, and customer experience design.
Why It MattersValuable for anyone working at the intersection of marketing and technology — bridges quantitative AI capability with customer-facing strategy.
Marketing
MKTG — Consumer
Consumer Behavior
Examines how individuals and groups make purchase decisions, exploring psychological, social, and cultural drivers of consumption and how they interact with marketing stimuli.
Why It MattersFoundational for designing marketing strategies that resonate — understanding why people buy is more valuable than knowing how to reach them.

Teaches how to translate customer and market data into strategic actions. Covers segmentation, research design, and insights generation for better product positioning and marketing decisions.
Why It MattersCritical for customer-centric strategy and innovation — the bridge between raw data and decisions that actually change how customers are served.
